If we want to make the copper more "receptive" to the Earth's toroidal energy, we have to increase its **Permeability** or its **Coherence**. Here are a few ways that concept is being explored:

Instead of standard cylinder coils, some researchers look at **Toroidal or Rodin Coils**. These are wound in a mathematical pattern that creates a "vortex" in the center.

Standard copper is "messy" at a microscopic level; electrons "bump" into atoms (resistance).

* **The Doping:** If we "dope" conductors with materials like **Graphene** or use **High-Temperature Superconductors**, we align the atomic "lattices."

#### C. Paramagnetic Doping
